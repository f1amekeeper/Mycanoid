A race of ambulatory fungus creatures. They were known for their peacefulness and appreciation of quiet, making their homes in the darker corners of the world.

Mycanoid Features

Source: Tyler's sourcebook

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 2.
  • Age. Mycanoid have lifespans equivalent to humans.
  • Alignment. Mycanoids tend toward Good alignments, as they focus on togetherness and peace.
  • Speed. Your base walking speed is 20 feet.
  • Superior Darkvision. Your darkvision has a range of 120 feet, instead of 60.
  • Sunlight Sickness. You have disadvantage on ability checks and Constitution saving throws in direct sunlight.
  • Spore Speech. You communicate wordlessly and do not require vocal components for spells. You talk in a manner similar to telepathy in a range up to 120 feet away. you cannot communicate with creatures that lack a physical body such as elementals and spirits.
  • Fungal Body. Your Mushroom body is resilient and sturdy, you have resistance to necrotic damage, and advantage on any checks against poisons.
  • Specialized Metabolism. Because of your feeding habits, you can consume poisoned, diseased, and rotting food without issue.
  • Mushroom Talent. You have proficiency in the Medicine, and Survival Skills.
  • Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common, and Undercommon

Worker

Source: Tyler's Sourcebook

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity score increases by 1.
  • Size. Mycanoids workers are among the smallest and remain Small throughout their lifespan.
  • Pacifying Spores. As the smallest of the Mycanoids, Workers still by no means are defenseless. they can make a single special ranged attack as a bonus action once per long rest. the target makes a Con sav with a DC equal to 8 + Prof + Con mod, being uneffected on success. The target becomes stunned for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns.
  • Workers Pride. You take great pride in your work and reject failure. Once per long rest you may reroll one check made that uses artisans tools. You make this reroll using advantage.
  • Workers Instinct. You have proficiency with one artisan tool of your choice. at 5th level your body has adapted to the job you you gain expertise in the tool.

Fighter

Source: Tyler's Sourcebook

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Strength score increases by 1.
  • Size. Mycanoids fighters are among the biggest of Mycanoids. Just as all other mycanoids, they are born Small. once they reach 5th level, the undergo a growth spurt to become Medium growing 2d6 feet taller.
  • Melting Spores. As the main combat force they are equipped with spores to aid them in combat. when making a melee weapon attack they can add 1d6 necrotic damage to its strikes, once per turn.
  • Growth Spurt. A Warrior of the mycanoids can undergo a sudden change in its physiology allowing it to grow larger as an action. Increase 1 size category, while your size is increased in this way you have disadvantage on all dexterity checks and saves. you may do this once per long rest and lasts for 1 minute.
  • Fighters Instinct. You have proficiency with one martial weapon of your choice.

Sovereign

Source: Tyler's Sourcebook

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Charisma score increases by 1.
  • Size. Mycanoids Sovereigns are the average size of a Mycanoid. Just as all other mycanoids, they are born Small. once they reach 5th level, the undergo a growth spurt to become Medium growing 2d4 feet taller.
  • Guiding Spores. As the leaders of the mycanoids their spores help them lead groups in turn. When rolling for initiative, you may grant up to half of your initiative to your allies, distributing it as you wish. lose the amount of initiative you give out.
  • Caring leader. When an ally is downed within sight, you may add your level to the damage of your next damage roll against one target.
  • Leaders Instinct. You make persuasion and insight checks with advantage.

Rotpriest

Source: Tyler's Sourcebook

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Wisdom score increases by 1.
  • Size. Mycanoids Rotpriests are the average size of a Mycanoid. Just as all other mycanoids, they are born Small. once they reach 5th level, the undergo a growth spurt to become Medium growing 2d4 feet taller.
  • Planning Nature. On long rest declare one skill. The next time you roll that skill check you roll 1 extra d20 for the roll.
  • Arcane Spores. When casting a spell your spores follow. After hitting a creature with a spell, you may cast your next spell as if from the target you hit. If the spell has touch you may treat it as a range of 5 feet. you may not use this feature for spells that target Self.
  • Rotpriests Instinct. You gain proficiency in arcana and have advantage on arcana checks
